Steven's Home Healthcare

At Stevens Home Health Care we recognize the enormous emotional, physical and financial strains imposed upon the client and family when faced with a care giving crisis. We can offer viable solutions to ease your burdens. We bring to the client and family an impeccable reputation for providing superior care and peace of mind, knowing that you and your loved one are in good hands.

As an important source of assistance to the client and the family, Steven Home Health Care is sensitive to your needs and desires. We are "in touch" with your emotional and medical requirements and we stop at nothing to assist in any way we can. We approach each client with the care and concern that you expect. ... and we demand.

Stevens Home Health Care personnel are highly trained experienced professionals. You can feel confident that whatever the need, from grooming and routine hygiene to tracheostomy care and pain control, the Stevens Home health Care professionals are there to relive the stresses of both client and family.

With Stevens Home Health Care you can choose from several available programs that are specifically developed to meet your needs, from the simplest custodial care to highly skilled nursing care. The choice is yours and the quality of service is always second to none.

Skilled Care

Not everyone needs the same type of care. Some clients require more care, some less care. But when skilled nursing care is indicated Stevens Home Health Care is there to offer assistance.

Our Registered Nurses (RNs) and Licensed Practical Nurses (LPNs) are experienced professional who assist the client with all of their physical and emotional needs. Our Director of Nursing and our RNs continually supervise both the client and staff to insure that the best possible care is provided to our client.

Our Skilled Nursing Program includes:

  • Initial and periodic assessments to evaluate client and family home needs.
  • Performing skilled, specialized procedures under the physician's treatment plan.
  • Administering injections.
  • Instructing and supervising the client and the family regarding the client's medical requirements.
  • Providing emotional support and assistance.

Personal Care

Working under the supervision of our RNs and LPNs our qualified and experienced Home Health Aides provide "hands-on" assistance including:

Assisting with normal activities of daily living such as dressing, grooming, personal hygiene, simple skin care and eating.

  • Bathing - tub, shower or bed.
  • Special skin care as directed.
  • Active and passive exercises.
  • Assisting with ambulation and transfers (walking and moving form bed to chair, etc.). Meal preparation.

  • Light housekeeping and shopping.

Homemaker

Simple custodial care is available through Stevens Home Health Care. Our Homemakers will provide the housekeeping, cokking, shopping and laundry services that will make your day a little easier. Built into the program is just the flexibility that you need, including:

  • Assisting the client toward independent living.
  • Preparing and serving meals.
  • Assisting with household management.
  • Shopping for groceries.
  • Accompanying the client to appointments.
  • Doing the client's laundry.
  • General housekeeping.

Respite

Respite care provided by skilled Stevens Home Health Care personnel affords the family an opportunity to receive a much needed break. Respite care is available for a few hours, days or longer. Respite care will help the family maintain a more normal life style by:

  • Providing rest or personal time.
  • Enabling a family member to more meaningful time with the client.
  • Enabling the family to take a needed rest or vacation.
  • Allowing family members time to focus on important areas.
